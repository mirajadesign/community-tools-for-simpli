❓ What is this? Is my data private?

What it is: a free calculator to estimate and track what your Simpli Studio setup costs each month — the plan, your generation keys, and any other tools.

Does it take payment info? No. There are no accounts, no logins, and no credit cards, and nothing is ever charged.

Where does my data go? Nowhere. Everything runs in your own browser and is saved only on your device. Nothing is sent to us or anyone else — clearing your browser data clears it.

What is “Track actual spend”? An optional place to type the numbers shown on your Simpli Plan & Billing page, just to compare reality against the estimate. It does not connect to Simpli and takes no payment details.

Why can a video cost about $0? In Simpli a video is built from images, so your fal key pays for the images; stitching them into the finished video is essentially free on your key (it draws on your plan’s video-minutes instead). That’s why this tool prices a video by the images it uses — set Images/vid and the cost follows.

Is this official? No — it’s unofficial and community-made, not affiliated with, endorsed by, or released by Simpli Studio.

1 Your Pages what you make

A Page is one brand/account in Simpli (it shows in your Page switcher). Add a row per Page and set what it makes each day: narrated videos, static image posts / banners, and animations. Videos use your plan's monthly minute cap; images and animations run on your fal key only. A video’s dollar cost is really the images it’s built from (its frames) plus voice, so a video can read ~$0 on its own while the images it uses carry the spend.
