1 Your Pages what you make

A Page is one brand or account in Simpli Studio (it shows in your Page switcher). Add a card for each Page, then list what it makes as content lines: reels built from images, image posts, animations, lip-sync videos, or prompt-to-video. Each line has its own daily count, length, and quality, so one Page can make a 60s reel, two 30s reels, and a Best-quality lip-sync special side by side. Reels, lip-sync, and prompt-to-video use your plan's monthly video-minutes; image posts and animations run on your keys.

2 Other monthly costs don't forget these

The stuff that quietly adds up: a scheduler, a community membership, an LLM subscription or two, a virtual assistant (VA). Fill in what you actually pay and leave the rest at $0, so the total tells you the truth.

❓ FAQs
Generation keys such as fal, ElevenLabs, and Fish Audio are billed through your accounts, separately from your Simpli Studio subscription. Simpli Studio meters narrated video minutes, while images and animations run on your keys. Tablio works inside Claude or as a standalone page, and everything you enter stays on your device.

Tablio is built by a fellow creator to help the Simpli Faceless community. It works alongside Simpli Studio to make your costs easier to see and manage. It's an independent project, not officially affiliated with Simpli Faceless or Simpli Studio. I hope it helps!
