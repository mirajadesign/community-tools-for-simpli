Two ways to use it

Both are free and both stay on your machine. The only difference is how much of the work the tool does for you.

1

Open it in your browser

Click the button and it opens in any browser. Set up your Pages and you have your numbers. Nothing to install, no account, and it works entirely on its own. This is the full calculator.

Open the cost tracker →
2

Use it inside Claude does more

Everything the browser version does, plus Claude sets it up for you, keeps it updated on its own, and can read your Simpli Studio billing page to fill in your real spend automatically. If you already use Claude, this is the stronger option. Paste these two lines in, both free:

# add it for free, then install /plugin marketplace add mirajadesign/community-tools-for-simpli /plugin install cost-tracker-for-simpli

Needs a paid Claude plan. The automatic spend fill runs on the official Claude for Chrome extension, so you'll need that installed and turned on.

Why I made Tablio

I built Tablio because I needed it. I've spent real money testing tools for faceless AI content, and the monthly cost gets scattered across subscriptions, video-minute limits, image generation, AI voice, API usage, and all the extras.

James has built an amazing platform with Simpli Studio. But I also wanted one clear place to see what the rest of my content creation stack costs each month, plan for the volume I want to create, and compare that estimate with what I actually spent.

Tablio gave me the full picture I was missing, so I decided to share it. I'm just another creator learning as I go, building useful things, and giving something back to a great community. I hope it helps!

Tablio is an independent tool built by a member of the Simpli Faceless community. It works alongside Simpli Studio to help creators estimate and track their full content-production costs. Tablio is not an official product of Simpli Faceless or Simpli Studio.
